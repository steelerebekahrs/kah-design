A catalogue for an exhibition which examines 1930s Manhattan. It outlines the contrast and opposition between the ideology of the state — a vision of future, wealth, power and success — and the reality of the extreme poverty being experienced by the public. These opposing viewpoints are represented through colour, type and manipulated images.

Workhouse of the New Era.
