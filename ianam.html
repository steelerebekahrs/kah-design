Title sequence for the short film I Am Not A Monster — a psychological horror that follows a young man who is haunted by a violent demonic-faced growth on the base of his head and his battle to differenciate between his own voice and the voice of this monster.
The theme of the film is subtly reflected in the typography by the use of two very different typefaces diverging from one point. The visual language of the title sequence is then carried through to poster, packaging and print.

I Am Not A Monster — Film Titles.
Client: Conor Byrne & Grace Quilligan.
Disciplines: Motion, Typography, Packaging, Print, Sound.
Date: May 2018.
