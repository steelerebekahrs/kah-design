MoTyf — Fesitval Identity.

Brief
An identity for the international MoTyf Typography Festival, this year taking place in the DLR Lexicon Dun Laoghaire and focussing on type in motion. To suggest movement while creating a tool to display information, the ‘o’, was extended using it as a container for information or to move across items.


Disciplines
Identity, Environment, Wayfinding, Print.

Client
Hillary Kenna.
