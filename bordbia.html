A window display and campaign for Bord Bia's Consumer Lifestyle Trends. The challenge was to explain how Bord Bia uses their exstensive research in worldwide lifestyle trends to identify potential markets for Irish food brands around the world. We took their dense statistics and made them relatable and accessible for everyone through fun copyrighting and infographics. This project was chosen as winner and was commisioned by Bord Bia to be taken forward.
